# Neuropathic Pain
Can be caused in cases of neuronal damage, in which a retraction bulb is formed and over-expresses Î±2 receptors, which will lead to pain. There can also exist phenomena of ephatic crosstalk, in which neurons are activated by the lack of myelin isolation and protection.
