# Meninges

3 meningeal layers, which cover the SNS and PNS:

- [[Dura matter]] (endosteal and meningeal layers)
- [[Arachnoid mater]] (with the [[subarachnoid space]])
- [[Pia matter]]

These fold in the CNS (e.g. [[Falx cerebri]]) but not the PNS.
